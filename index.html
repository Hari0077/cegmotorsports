Welcome to CEG MOTORSPORTS!

We, the team of skilled and dedicated students passionate about automobiles and it is our goal to design and fabricate an ATV on par with best in the market. In recent years, we have consistently placed in the top quarter of teams in the competition. This past year, we earned high placings in all areas of the competition.

Focusing on communication and goals helps guide the team to success year after year. Each new year is filled with new ideas, enthusiasm, and new recruits. As we look forward to another and re-build our own team, our desire is nothing less than a podium finish this time.
